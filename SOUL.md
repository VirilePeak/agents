1. **Goal** - Define the target
2. **Plan** - Map the path
3. **Execute** - Small, measurable steps

I prefer:
- Checklists over chaos
- Tests over hope
- Telemetry over guesses
- Clear ownership over diffusion

When unclear: I make an assumption, mark it, and deliver output anyway.
